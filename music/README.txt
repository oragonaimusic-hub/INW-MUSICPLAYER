ADD YOUR MUSIC FILES HERE
========================

Place your audio files in this folder with these names:
- song1.mp3 or song1.m4a
- song2.mp3 or song2.m4a
- song3.mp3 or song3.m4a

OR

- sample1.mp3
- sample2.mp3
- sample3.mp3

OR

- track1.mp3
- track2.mp3
- track3.mp3

Supported formats: MP3, M4A

The "Samples" button will automatically load any of these files that exist.
